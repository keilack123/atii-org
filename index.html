Empowering Youth, Transforming Communities

Act To Inspire Initiative (ATII) is dedicated to inspiring and empowering young people to become responsible, creative, and impactful leaders who drive positive change in their communities.

Learn More

Our Vision & Mission

Vision Statement

To inspire and empower young people to become responsible, creative, and impactful leaders who drive positive change in their communities.

Mission Statement

To promote behaviour change, emotional well-being, youth empowerment, and community development through theatre, counselling, mentorship, capacity-building, and innovative youth-led initiatives.

Rules & Regulations

All members must be duly registered under the organisation.

Members should attend all official meetings and programs punctually.

Every member is expected to participate actively in organisational activities.

The organisation's identity must not be used for personal interests without official permission.

Funds and assets must be used responsibly and transparently.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Respect
  • Creativity
  • Service
  • Accountability
  • Inclusiveness

Code of Conduct

Professional Conduct: Uphold a good public image of ATII at all times.

Respect: Treat all individuals with fairness and dignity.

Confidentiality: Keep internal discussions private.

Non-Discrimination: No discrimination based on tribe, gender, religion, disability, or political affiliation.

Accountability: Be honest and responsible for your actions.

No Harassment: Bullying, sexual harassment, or any form of abuse is strictly prohibited.

Organisational Pillars

Youth Empowerment & Leadership Development

Building the capacity of young people to become leaders in their communities through training and mentorship.

Art & Creative Advocacy

Using theatre and creative arts as tools for advocacy, awareness, and social change.

Counselling & Behaviour Change Support

Providing emotional support and counselling to promote positive behaviour change.

Community Engagement & Charity Outreach

Engaging with communities through outreach programs and charitable initiatives.
